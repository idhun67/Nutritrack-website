What is it

Your nutrition.
Finally personalised.

Most people eat without knowing what their body actually needs. AI Nutrition Tracker fixes that.

Enter your profile once — age, weight, height, goal and lifestyle — and the app instantly calculates your personalised daily targets for calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber and five essential vitamins using the clinically validated Mifflin-St Jeor formula and WHO / ICMR dietary guidelines. No generic charts. No guesswork. Just science-backed numbers built around you.

Track every meal. Hit every target.

Log what you eat by searching the built-in food database or entering meals manually. After every meal, the app analyses what you've consumed and recommends specific foods to fill the gaps — short on protein? It suggests grilled chicken. Low on Vitamin C? An orange. Running behind on carbs? Brown rice. Smart, specific, and instant.

The science

Not guesswork.
Peer-reviewed formulas.

Your calorie target is calculated from your actual body measurements using the most accurate BMR formula available, then adjusted for how you live.

Mifflin-St Jeor BMR
WHO micronutrient guidelines
ICMR dietary reference intakes
Harris-Benedict activity factors
Mifflin-St Jeor formula
BMR = 10 × weight(kg)
     + 6.25 × height(cm)
     − 5 × age(years)
     + 5 (male) / −161 (female)
1
Calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) from body measurements
2
Multiply by activity factor (1.2 → 1.725) to get TDEE
3
Adjust: −500 kcal (lose), 0 (maintain), +300 kcal (gain)
