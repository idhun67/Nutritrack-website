Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🎯
Live calorie ring
A circular progress arc shows calories consumed vs your daily goal in real time — updates the moment you log a meal.
📸
AI photo food detection
Snap a photo of your meal and LogMeal AI automatically identifies the food items and adds their nutritional information in seconds.
💡
Time-aware meal plan
After each meal the app checks your remaining 9-nutrient needs and shows a full meal plan for the rest of the day — breakfast, lunch, snacks and dinner — tailored to the time and your diet.
🥚
Eggs measured by pieces
Egg items are automatically measured by number of pieces, not grams. All values are USDA FoodData Central verified.
💧
Water tracker
Tap cups to log water. Your daily goal is auto-calculated from your body weight (35ml per kg).
💊
Vitamin dashboard
Progress bars for Vitamins A, B1, C, D and B12. Colour-coded green / amber / red so you know at a glance what needs attention.
⚖️
Weight tracker with history
Log your weight daily and see the trend over time. Delete incorrect entries. Profile weight auto-updates and recalculates all targets.
📋
30-day meal history
Every day's meals are saved automatically. Browse any past date to see exactly what you ate and your macro totals for that day.
🌏
Timezone setting
Set your timezone (IST, GMT, PST and 19 others) so meal time detection and daily history are always accurate wherever you are.

The science

Not guesswork.
Peer-reviewed formulas.

Your calorie target is calculated from your actual body measurements using the most accurate BMR formula available, then adjusted for how active you are and what your goal is.

Mifflin-St Jeor BMR
WHO micronutrient guidelines
ICMR dietary reference intakes
USDA FoodData Central
Mifflin-St Jeor formula
BMR = 10 × weight(kg)
     + 6.25 × height(cm)
     − 5 × age(years)
     + 5 (male) / −161 (female)
1
Calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) from body measurements
2
Multiply by activity factor (1.2 → 1.725) to get TDEE
3
Adjust: −500 kcal (lose), 0 (maintain), +300 kcal (gain)
